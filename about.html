About the Project

This project applies advanced computer vision and artificial intelligence to empower farmers with real-time crop monitoring. By analyzing images of crops, our system identifies early signs of pest infestations, nutrient deficiencies, and diseases—enabling fast, data-driven action.

Project Objectives

  • Develop an AI model that detects crop health anomalies through image analysis.
  • Provide an intuitive platform for uploading and viewing crop imagery.
  • Offer clear, actionable recommendations to improve productivity and reduce crop loss.
  • Automate routine inspection tasks, saving both time and labor.

Motivation

Traditional methods of monitoring large fields are inefficient, often missing early warning signs that cost farmers dearly. Our motivation is to replace this reactive approach with a smart, proactive solution that’s scalable, accessible, and effective across different farm sizes and crop types.

Team & Technologies

Our multidisciplinary team—“Talent Farm”—blends expertise in AI, agriculture, and software development. The technologies we’ve chosen support both innovation and scalability:

  • Python & OpenCV: Core tools for computer vision image processing.
  • TensorFlow: Framework for training and deploying deep learning models.
  • Web Stack (HTML, CSS, JS): Used for building the responsive user interface.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Supports secure storage and easy access to model outputs and reports.

Key Features

AI-Powered Crop Disease Detection

Our model analyzes leaf images to detect various diseases like blight, rust, and mildew with high accuracy, enabling timely treatment.

Automated Pest Identification

Detect pest types and severity automatically from crop images, helping reduce pesticide overuse.

Growth Stage Classification

Classifies the growth stage of crops and advises on irrigation, fertilization, and harvesting times.

User-Friendly Dashboard

Farmers can upload images easily and receive instant reports along with historical trends and recommendations.

Scalable Cloud Infrastructure

Data and models are hosted on cloud servers ensuring high availability and fast processing for farmers worldwide.
